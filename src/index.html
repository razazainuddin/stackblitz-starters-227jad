Psycho Bunny Case Study

Psycho Bunny, a leading high-quality clothing company, has tripled its business in the past two years and is committed to delivering the best products and services to its customers. Due to this massive growth trajectory, the company required a new order management system (OMS) to keep up with their rapid expansion of stores and the resulting increase in demand. Bringing in the best solutions from partners: AWS Fluent Commerce Informatica Pivotree strategized, architected, and implemented an order management system (OMS) with data management integration that would be powerful enough to process the anticipated order volume under an aggressive 6-month timeline so that Psycho Bunny would continue to delight their customers.
